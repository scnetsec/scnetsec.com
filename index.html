Color Harmony

Color Harmony I went through many different color pairings, trying both tertiary and analogous colors until I finally decided to go with a nice tertiary blend. I choose the colors yellow-orange, blue-violet and yellow- green. I started building a pyramid and had a really nice idea going but decided along the way that I really wanted to try something new. So I got out the gradient tool and really spent some time learning how to get it to look really nice. I played with levels and my end product was far beyond anything I expected from myself. For my title I played with some electric lightning effects and drop shadows and was satisfied with what I had in the end.

Text Face

My eye shape was created using the Modesto Open font. First I stretched out the “O.” Next, I selected Type>Create Outlines. And then Object>Compound Path>Release. Quick reminder, if you don’t release compound path, the text area tool will not work. Next, I filled the “O” with tiny 1’s and 0’s from Modesto and reduced stroke to zero. I then selected the completed eye and copied it into position. I added one more Modesto Open “O,” changed the fill to white, then copied 2 of them into my eyes. Inside the whites, I added a filled in “O” from Roboto for the blacks of the eyes. At some point I added some glasses for this dude, The frames came from a capital “I.” And the lenses from a capital “O.” Both are from the nunito family..
